Of all the teem libraries, nrrd has benefitted most from the work other people.

  • David Weinstein, J. Dean Brederson, Milan Ikits, Vidya Elangovan, and Joe Kniss were early adopters/debuggers.
  • Dave Weinstein added nrrd support into SCIRun
  • Milan Ikits got teem to compile on Windows, and ensures that new features in my GNU makefiles have a workable analog in Visual Studio.
  • Jonathan Blocksom added support for Mac OSX.
  • Milan Ikits and James Bigler added support for gzip compression.
  • Milan Ikits added support for bzip2 compression.
