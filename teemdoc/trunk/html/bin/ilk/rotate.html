  ilk: Rotations

Rotating

It is often important to set the origin when doing rotations, since the origin is the one point you know will stay put: 
ilk -i init.ppm -0 u:0,0 -t rotate:30 | tojpg > rot1.jpg

ilk -i init.ppm -0 u:0.5,0.5 -t rotate:30 | tojpg > rot2.jpg

ilk -i init.ppm -0 u:1,1 -t rotate:30 | tojpg > rot3.jpg
